## Overview

Version 0.7.0 represents the completion of the first major intelligence architecture for RT-DOS.

The platform has evolved from a collection of analysis engines into a structured Trading Decision Intelligence Platform with a layered intelligence pipeline.

---

## Major Features

### Intelligence Pipeline

Introduced a centralized Intelligence Pipeline responsible for orchestrating all analysis engines.

---

### Market Regime Engine

Classifies the overall market environment to provide context for trading decisions.

---

### Institutional Confidence Engine

Calculates the Institutional Confidence Index (ICI) to estimate the quality and reliability of each trading opportunity.

---

### Probability Engine

Introduces probability-based trade assessment including:

- Probability of Success
- Expected Move
- Holding Period
- Risk / Reward

---

### User Interface

Improvements include:

- Market Command Centre V2
- Opportunity Radar V3
- Executive Ribbon
- Status Ribbon
- Workspace Framework

---

## Architecture

Current architecture:

Market Data

→ Market Regime
→ Institutional Confidence
→ Probability
→ Decision Engine
→ Presentation Layer
